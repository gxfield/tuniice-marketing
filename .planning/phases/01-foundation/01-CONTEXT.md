## Implementation Decisions

### Neo-brutalist style direction
- Neo-brutalist (polished) — brutalist bones with modern polish. Bold shapes and high contrast but cleaner spacing and smoother edges. Think Linear or Gumroad's recent aesthetic, not raw concrete brutalism.
- Color palette: dark background, white text, neon green primary accent, with warm secondary accents (amber, orange) for depth. Not monochrome — the warm tones add variety without diluting the brand.
- Typography: mixed approach — bold sans-serif for headings (Impact, Archivo Black style), monospace for body text and code/functional elements. Contrast between display and functional text.
- Borders/shadows: solid color offset shadows (not blurred). Cards and buttons appear to "float" with a hard shadow shifted down-right. This is the defining visual pattern — classic neo-brutalist.
